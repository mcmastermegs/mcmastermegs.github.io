Mechanical Engineering Graduate Society (MEGS)

The Mechanical Engineering Graduate Society (MEGS) is a student-run group committed to building a strong and supportive mechanical graduate student community.

About Us

The Mechanical Engineering Graduate Society (MEGS) serves as the representative body for all graduate students in mechanical and biomedical engineering at McMaster University. Our organization is dedicated to fostering academic excellence, professional development, and community building among graduate students pursuing advanced degrees in engineering disciplines.
